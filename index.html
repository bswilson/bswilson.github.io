About Scott

Currently, I'm working as a Program & Operations Manager with IBM Security's X-Force Red team. I have over 25 years of defining, planning, building/implementing, assessing, and auditing security systems, strategies and policies for my customers.

I have great written and oral communications skills, including authoring, presenting, and publishing clear, concise, and thorough information to executives and customers. I have experience with detailed root cause analysis, risk assessment, and program management tasks. I work effectively and efficiently both individually and as a team member in a professional environment.

Areas of expertise I have cultivated include security architecture, systems integration, network design, compliance, auditing, penetration testing, risk evaluation and assessments.
