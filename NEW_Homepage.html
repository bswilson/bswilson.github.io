Welcome

I'm a 25-year cybersecurity professional, who currently manages multiple clients' Vulnerability Management programs, Penetration Testing engagements, and Adversarial Simulation projects at IBM X-Force Red, IBM Security's offensive security team.

I provide ongoing consulting for program improvement to executives, program leads, and other customer groups. I'm responsible for the day-to-day operations of the X-Force Red Team including customer management, program support, financial review and oversight, and internal process development and maturity efforts. I enjoy consulting with customers who are growing and developing vulnerability management and penetration testing programs of varying maturity levels.
